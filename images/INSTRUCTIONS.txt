# Profile Picture Instructions

## How to Add Your Photo:

1. **Take or choose a professional photo**
   - Headshot or casual professional photo works best
   - Good lighting, clear face
   - Neutral or solid color background recommended
   - Square ratio preferred (will be cropped to square)

2. **Name your photo:**
   - Rename it to: `profile.jpg` or `profile.png`
   
3. **Place it in this folder:**
   - Put your `profile.jpg` in the `images/` folder
   - Replace the placeholder

4. **Size recommendations:**
   - At least 500x500 pixels
   - Under 2MB file size for fast loading

## If you don't have a photo yet:
- A placeholder will show until you add your photo
- The site will still work perfectly!
- You can add it later anytime

## Tips for best results:
- Smile! It makes you approachable
- Professional but friendly
- Make sure your face is well-lit
- Avoid busy backgrounds

---

Your photo will appear in the "About" section with:
- Rounded corners
- A subtle shadow
- Hover animation
- "Available for hire" badge
